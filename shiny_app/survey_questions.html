Demographic Questions
  1. Have you taken a gap year?
  2. Where are you living this semester?
  3. If you are living on campus, are you in a single or do you have roommates/suitemates?
  4. Which gender identity do you most identify with?
  5. Specify your race and/or ethnicity.
  6. Which pre-orientation program, if any, did you participate in?
  7. Are you involved with any sports teams on campus?
Social Connection Questions
  1. List the names of the four people you are closest friends with in the Class of 2024 and specify where/how you first met each of them.
  2. How do you stay in contact with these people? (If multiple options apply, please pick the primary one.)
  3. How many people have you met virtually that you now often meet up with in-person? (Please enter “NA” if this does not apply to you.)
  4. In your opinion, which of the following is best for forming meaningful connections?
  5. When you do meet with people in-person, where do you go or what do you do? (Please remember that this survey is completely anonymous and your answers will not be used to get you in trouble.)
  6. Name the person in the Class of 2024 who you think is the most socially connected.
  7. How satisfied are you with your social connections with other first-year students at Harvard?
